## Style Prompt

Use the existing HTML demo as the source of truth: warm Intercom-like cream canvas, paper panels, black-brown ink, hand-built product UI, and friendly OpenPeeps characters. The video should feel like a thoughtful product walkthrough rather than a hype reel: human, calm, editorial, and precise. Motion should behave like pages and cards being gently arranged on a desk, with a few UI pulses for the Agent workflow.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#fbf7ef`
- Paper: `#fffdf8`
- Ink: `#26231f`
- Muted text: `#6f685f`
- Warm accent: `#d89a45`
- Soft warm fill: `#fff1c7`
- Trust green: `#3f8a71`
- Zhihu blue accent: `#1772f6`

## Typography

- Display: `"Songti SC", "STSong", serif`
- UI/body: `"PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Data labels: `"IBM Plex Mono", "SFMono-Regular", monospace`

## Motion

- Primary transition: warm paper wipe / push slide, 0.35-0.5s.
- Accent transition: soft overexposure flash for the product reveal.
- Entrances: staggered card rises, gentle scale, short UI pulses.
- Ambient motion: slow drift of lines, dots, and character clusters with finite GSAP repeats.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use dark cyber/AI styling.
- Do not present AI as a factual source or as the author themself.
- Do not imply private messaging, real-time author replies, or identity inference.
- Do not replace the demo's warm product surface with generic SaaS blue gradients.
